FAQs


Q: What time do gates open each day?

A: Gates open at 10:00 AM on Friday &Saturday & Sunday. With performances starting at 11:00 AM.

Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks?

A: Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but we have a wide selection of gourmet food trucks and beverage vendors on site.

Q: Are pets allowed at the festival?

A: We love our furry friends, but for the safety and comfort of all attendees, pets are not allowed at the festival. but service animals are welcome.

Q: Is there parking available at the venue?

A: Limited parking is available on-site, but we encourage attendees to carpool, bike, or use public transportation to reduce traffic and emissions.

Q: What should I bring to the festival?

A: We recommend bringing sunscreen, a hat, a reusable water bottle, and comfortable shoes. Don't forget your dancing shoes!

Q: Can I bring my camera to the festival?

A: Yes, feel free to capture your favorite moments at the festival! We encourage you to share your photos and experiences on social media using the hashtag #PulsewaveFestival.

Q: What safety measures are in place at the festival?

A: The safety and well-being of our attendees are our top priorities. We have security personnel, medical staff, and emergency services on-site to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all.

Q: Will tickets be available at the gate?

A: A limited number of tickets may be available at the gate, but we recommend purchasing tickets in advance to secure your spot.

Q: What is the refund policy?

A: Tickets are non-refundable, but they are transferable. If you are unable to attend, you may give your ticket to a friend or family member. Please contact us for more information. However, if the event is canceled due to unforeseen circumstances, refunds or ticket transfers will be offered.
