Folk & Indie Festival 2025

Celebrate music, art, and community at the Folk & Indie Festival! Experience amazing performances, local artisans, and delicious food surrounded by nature.

Festival Details

Zilker Park, Austin, TX 2100 Barton Springs Rd, Austin, TX 78704

Dates: Friday July 20/2025 to Sunday July 22/2025

Three unforgettable days of music and fun!

Ticket Prices

  1. Single-Day Pass: $45
  2. Weekend Pass (All 3 Days): $120
  3. VIP Pass: $200 (Includes premium seating, backstage access, and complimentary drinks)
  4. Kids under 12: Free entry!
