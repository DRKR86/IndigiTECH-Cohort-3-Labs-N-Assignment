Welcome to Pulsewave Festival!

Our Story


The Folk & Indie Festival is organized by a team of passionate music lovers, event planners, and community advocates, all deeply connected to the heart of Austin, Texas. With years of experience in event production, curating music, and fostering local talent, our team is united by a shared vision: to create a festival that embodies the spirit of Austin—a city known for its rich music culture, creative expression, and inclusivity. Our mission is simple: to provide a platform for independent musicians and artists, to create an environment where attendees can enjoy world-class performances, connect with local creators, and experience the magic of Austin’s live music scene in a welcoming, family-friendly atmosphere. From booking incredible indie and folk artists to designing an eco-conscious festival, we strive to put our values into every aspect of the event. We believe in supporting the local economy, which is why we prioritize working with Austin-based food vendors, artisans, and small businesses. By doing this, we not only provide a stage for independent artists but also celebrate the diverse talent in the Austin community. Our team has a strong belief in fostering inclusivity and sustainability. We are committed to making the festival accessible to people of all backgrounds, and we provide programs and activities for all ages, from toddlers to adults. We also take our environmental responsibility seriously—our team works hard to reduce waste, use sustainable materials, and create a festival that aligns with Austin’s forward-thinking, eco-friendly culture.

Our Mission

Our History


The Folk & Indie Festival began in 2 as a small gathering of music enthusiasts and local artists, looking to bring a fresh, authentic vibe to Austin’s rich festival landscape. What started as a humble event has grown over the years into one of the city's most anticipated celebrations of independent music and culture. With a focus on intimate performances, community engagement, and sustainable practices, the festival has become a beloved tradition for locals and visitors alike. Our team is proud to have played a role in shaping the festival into what it is today, and we look forward to many more years of music, connection, and creativity.

Meet the Team


Our team is made up of individuals who live and breathe Austin's music scene. Whether we’re curating the best indie performers, organizing community events, or supporting the festival’s sustainability initiatives, each member brings a unique passion to the festival. We’re here to ensure that every attendee leaves with memories, new music favorites, and a deeper connection to the spirit of Austin’s indie and folk culture.
