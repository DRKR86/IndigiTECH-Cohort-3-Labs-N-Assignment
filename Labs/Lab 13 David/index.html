Here’s a list of everything you’ll need to make delicious Chicken Alfredo:

For The Sauce

  1. Butter – Unsalted, for a rich and creamy base.
  2. Heavy Cream – Adds the creamy texture.
  3. Garlic – Minced, for flavor.
  4. Parmesan Cheese – Freshly grated for the best flavor.
  5. Salt and Pepper – To taste.

For the Chicken

  1. Chicken Breasts – Boneless, skinless (or thighs, if preferred).
  2. Olive Oil or Butter – For cooking the chicken.
  3. Italian Seasoning – Optional, for added flavor.

For the Pasta

  1. Fettuccine – The classic choice, but other pasta types work too.
  2. Water and Salt – For boiling the pasta.

Optional Garnishes

  1. Parsley – Fresh, chopped, for a touch of color.
  2. Lemon Zest – A hint of freshness.
  3. Red Pepper Flakes – For a little kick.

Tools You’ll Need

  1. Large pot (for boiling pasta).
  2. Skillet or sauté pan (for cooking the chicken and sauce).
  3. Whisk (for a smooth sauce).
  4. Tongs (for tossing pasta and serving).

With these essentials, you’ll be ready to create a creamy and flavorful Chicken Alfredo that’s sure to impress!
